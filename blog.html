Personal Productivity

I teach people how to become more productive in their work and life using a practical and holistic approach. Practical productivity begins by creating an operating system that manages all aspects of your life. The holistic aspect of productivity ensures you are managing more than your time – you are also managing for your energy and attention.

Continuous Improvement

I help organizations improve their processes for producing content, services, and products. Using a group of analytical tools including Lean Six Sigma, I determine the pain points and bottlenecks that hold back your organization’s full potential to produce its best work.

Project Management

I work with individuals, teams, and organizations to help them manage their projects by defining success and achieving it. I apply a variety of management processes including Agile and Scrum development to manage your workflow to deliver projects on time and within budget.

Analysis & Forecasting

I deliver data-fueled solutions for companies across sectors that include design, collection, analysis, visualization, and reporting of data. I employ cutting-edge analytics and machine learning to extract insight from data, then produce actionable information for organizations to make strategic data-driven decisions.

Operations Management

I work with organizations to maximize effectiveness by streamlining their operations from input to output. Using measurement, analysis, and planning, I help maximize resources and expand process control to create the best possible content, services, and products.

Public Speaking

I engage with audiences on topics including productivity, growth, work-life balance, and more. I have a passion for speaking to students that are interested in moving from academia to industry and governmental positions.
